Admission

The admissions to the UG and PG courses start soon after the results of H.S.C. and the final year Graduation are declared. Advertisement regarding the admissions for various courses of the institution is published in the regional and national (print and electronic) media; and the same is displayed on the Institutional Website http://www.zcoer.org The ‘Information Brochure’ is prepared and Hard copy of the ‘Information Brochure’ is provided to the students at the time of admission. The admission for all the courses are merit based. The college fallows the University guidelines and the procedures. There are no management quota admissions. Admissions to all courses are given on the basis of merit of their previous qualifying examination. For admission student have to take registration & admission form, from the college & has to submit filled registration & admission from along with necessary document required for admission. After registration, the admissions lists are published on merit basis. The admission process is completed in a big hall in the presence of all students and admissions are finalized on the same day same time. Though there is provision for management quota, institution has been admitting students on the basis of merit only, without utilizing management quota. Hence the whole admission process is transparent.

About Us

Welcome to Zeal College of Engineering & Research, Pune

Zeal Education Society is established in year 1996 by Hon. Shri S. M. Katkar, an eminent industrialist committed to noble cause of education. Society runs eight institutes from Pre-primary to PhD courses. It caters to educational needs of more than 7000 students, and it’s continuously expanding to cope up with growing demands in the field of technical education.

ZCOER is established in 2007 which symbolized the beginning of new era in technical education. The college offers UG engineering degree courses in Civil Engineering, Computer Engineering, and Electrical Engineering, Information Technology, Electronics & Telecommunication, Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Electronics & Computer Engineering and Mechanical Engineering. The college also offers PG courses in Electrical Engineering (Power System), Electronics & Telecommunication (IoT & Sensor Systems), Mechanical Engineering (ME Design / Robotics & Automation) and Master of Business Administration. The institute is also granted as PhD research center in Computer Engineering, Electronics & Telecommunication and Mechanical Engineering under Savitribai Phule Pune University, Pune (SPPU) since 2013.
